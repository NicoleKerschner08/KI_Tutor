Project description

Welcome to KI Tutor, an innovative project designed to empower learning through AI. Using the ChatGPT API, KI Tutor provides a personalized, interactive tutoring experience to enhance understanding and skills across subjects.

Our goals include making educational support accessible, exploring AI applications in learning, and empowering users to engage in problem-solving within a safe environment.

Through KI Tutor, users gain AI literacy, develop critical thinking skills, and enjoy self-paced learning opportunities, allowing them to revisit challenging topics and reinforce knowledge at their own speed.

KI Tutor connects users with ChatGPT, offering interactive AI support for learning and development.
